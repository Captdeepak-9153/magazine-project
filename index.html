Head of the Department’s Message

Welcome to the department of Computer Science and Engineering (Data Science) at GITA Autonomous College, Bhubaneswar. As head of the department, I am privileged to have this opportunity to introduce our family members. At our department, we are offering undergraduate program based on the cutting-edge technologies such as Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Cyber Security, and Data Science. This program has been designed to prepare the students to handle day to day data analytics problems along with development of software in intelligent way. Our curriculum is constantly updated based on the demands of industry and different data analytics problems being faced by research centers. We are committed to provide up-to-date technology to our students with the fundamentals and the knowledge they need to excel in their future careers. Our faculty members are renowned and experts in their own fields, and they provide highest quality education to our students. They are always committed to help students with proper guidance and support to make them technocrats of repute to sustain every field of life. Besides we offer a wide range of extracurricular activities and different national and international level events that provide students with opportunities to connect industry professionals and academicians to explore themselves with latest trends and developments in computer science. College has different student clubs independently run by the students where students can show their ability and technical under the leadership of expert faculty members. The department provides excellent infrastructures with latest software and high end laboratories to support the dynamic technology trends and to accelerate the research as the college is the best research hub of Biju Patnaik University of Technology. We take this opportunity to invite you to have physical realization. Jai Hind !!
Dr. N. K. Kamila
